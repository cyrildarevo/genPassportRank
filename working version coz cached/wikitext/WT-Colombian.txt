Visa requirements for holders of ordinary passports travelling for tourism purposes:

Colombia is an associated member of [[Mercosur]]. As such, its citizens enjoy unlimited access to any of the full members ([[Argentina]], [[Brazil]], [[Paraguay]] and [[Uruguay]]) and other associated members ([[Bolivia]], [[Chile]], [[Ecuador]] and [[Peru]]) with the right to residence and work, with no requirement other than nationality. Citizens of these nine countries (including Colombia) may apply for the grant of "temporary residence" for up to two years in another country of the bloc <ref> [http://www.mercosur.int/innovaportal/v/6581/3/innova.front/residir-e-trabalhar-no-mercosul "Residir e trabalhar no Mercosul" ] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620145643/http://www.mercosur.int/innovaportal/v/6581/3/innova.front/residir-e-trabalhar-no-mercosul |date=20 June 2017 }}, \\[[www.mercosur.int]]\\ </ref>. Then, they may apply for "permanent residence" just before the term of their "temporary residence" expires.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mre.gov.br/index.php?Itemid=548&id=1044&option=com_content&task=view|title=Perguntas mais freqüentes sobre integração regional e MERCOSUL|language=portuguese|publisher=MRE}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ansa.it/ansalatinabr/notizie/fdg/200907311350352763/200907311350352763.html|title=Paraguai assina acordos sobre migração no Mercosul|language=portuguese|publisher=ansa.it}}</ref>
