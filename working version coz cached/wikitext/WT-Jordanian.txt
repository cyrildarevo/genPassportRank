*Visas on arrival are available at [[Hamid Karzai International Airport]] to {{flag|United Nations}} employees, [[Interpol]] employees, business visitors, journalists, athletes, airline staff and passengers in transit from countries that do not have a [[List of diplomatic missions of Afghanistan|diplomatic mission of Afghanistan]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/asia/south-asia/afghanistan-to-issue-visas-on-arrival-to-cut-red-tape-boost-business|title=Afghanistan to issue visas on arrival to cut red tape, boost business|last=migration|date=2015-02-12|work=The Straits Times|access-date=2017-07-25|language=en}}</ref>
