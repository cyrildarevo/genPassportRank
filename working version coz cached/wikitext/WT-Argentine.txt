Visa requirements for holders of normal passports not travelling as journalists:

Argentina is a full member of [[Mercosur]]. As such, its citizens enjoy unlimited access to any of the other full members ([[Brazil]], [[Paraguay]], [[Uruguay]]) and associated members ([[Bolivia]], [[Chile]], [[Peru]], [[Colombia]], [[Ecuador]]) with the right to residence and work, with no requirement other than nationality. Citizens of these nine countries (including Argentina) may apply for the grant of "temporary residence" for up to two years in another country of the bloc <ref> [http://www.mercosur.int/innovaportal/v/6581/3/innova.front/residir-e-trabalhar-no-mercosul "Residir e trabalhar no Mercosul" ], \\[[www.mercosur.int]]\\ </ref>. Then, they may apply for "permanent residence" just before the term of their "temporary residence" expires.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mre.gov.br/index.php?Itemid=548&id=1044&option=com_content&task=view|title=Perguntas mais freqüentes sobre integração regional e MERCOSUL|language=portuguese|publisher=MRE}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ansa.it/ansalatinabr/notizie/fdg/200907311350352763/200907311350352763.html|title=Paraguai assina acordos sobre migração no Mercosul|language=portuguese|publisher=ansa.it}}</ref>
